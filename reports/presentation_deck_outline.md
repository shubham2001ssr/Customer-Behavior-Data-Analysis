## Slide 2: Executive Summary
**Project Goal:** To analyze a dataset of 5,000 retail transactions to uncover purchasing patterns, demographic segments, and drivers of revenue.

**Key Findings:**
1. **Seasonal Trends:** Revenue peaks significantly during the Winter season, driven by high-value Outerwear purchases.
2. **Demographic Dominance:** Millennials (age 25-34) account for the largest share of total transaction volume.
3. **Discount Strategy:** Applying discounts does not significantly increase Average Order Value (AOV); customers buy similarly priced items with or without promos.

## Slide 5: Actionable Business Recommendations
Based on the data analysis, I recommend the following strategic actions:

1. **Targeted Winter Campaigns:** Shift 15% of the marketing budget to promote high-margin Outerwear starting in early November.
2. **Subscription Push:** Since subscribers have higher LTV, introduce a "first month free" promotion at checkout to convert guest shoppers.
3. **Inventory Optimization:** Stock 20% more 'M' and 'L' sizes in coastal cities (NY & LA) to prevent stockouts during peak seasons.
4. **Re-evaluate Discount Strategy:** Test replacing flat discounts with "Buy One, Get One 50% Off" to artificially drive up the Average Order Value (AOV).
